One day, the cat went on a trip and left the rabbit alone. But then, a bird came along and offered to play with the rabbit. The rabbit was hesitant at first, but the bird promised to be gentle and kind. The rabbit agreed to play with the bird, and they had a great time chasing each other around and playing hide-and-seek. When the cat returned, he was furious to find the rabbit playing with someone else. He yelled at the rabbit and said he would never play with it again. But then, the bird came to the rescue and offered to play with the cat instead. The cat was hesitant at first, but the bird promised to be gentle and kind. The bird and the cat had a great time playing together, and the cat realized that having two friends was better than being jealous and
