 Ms. Rodriguez was the director of manufacturing for a leading tech company, specializing in the production of cutting-edge smartphones. In 2020, one of her engineers discovered a revolutionary new material that made the phones' screens virtually unbreakable. Excited by the potential of this innovation, Ms. Rodriguez was eager to implement it in their production line. However, during a demonstration of the new material, Ms. Rodriguez accidentally dropped her phone and it shattered into pieces. Horrified by the mishap, the engineer recommended a new protective feature to prevent future accidents. Ms. Rodriguez was so impressed with the feature that she immediately greenlit its development and implementation in their next production run. The accident served as a valuable lesson for Ms. Rodriguez, highlighting the importance of safety and innovation in the tech industry.
