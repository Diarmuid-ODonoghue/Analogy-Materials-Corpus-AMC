A fierce storm swept through the forest, and the peacock was caught in the rain, his beautiful feathers soaked and bedraggled. Just as he was about to give up hope, a group of friendly field mice came to his rescue. They worked together to build a cozy little nest for the peacock, and kept him warm and dry until the storm passed. The peacock was amazed at the kindness of the field mice, and he realized that he had been wrong to be so proud and standoffish.
