 A massive wildfire broke out in a dense forest, fueled by dry conditions and strong winds. The fire raged out of control, consuming everything in its path. Firefighters worked tirelessly to contain the blaze, but their efforts were hindered by a lack of water and the vast size of the fire. As the fire continued to spread, a team of experts was called in to help contain the blaze. They quickly realized that the only way to stop the fire was to use a massive amount of water, but they didn't have enough hoses to cover the entire area. They needed a solution to get the water to the fire quickly and efficiently. How can they put out the fire immediately?
