
Paulo and Giorgio have had great success with their pizza truck. Now they intend to fulfil their dream of getting a real Italian restaurant. One day, while looking through their books, Paulo discovered that Giorgio has always hidden the real amount of their profits, and that he actually keeps much more than half of it. Infuriated, Paulo decides to put an end to their collaboration. But since his wages depend on the collaboration with Giorgio, he changes his mind and finally continues working with him.
