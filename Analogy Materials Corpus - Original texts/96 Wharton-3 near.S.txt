Close Cue.
Dr. Popoff depended on his graduate student,
Sandy. However, he kept finding reasons not to upgrade his research
facilities because he was very cheap. After many fruitless
meetings, Sandy began inquiring around and was accepted
to work at a corporate laboratory. Dr. Popoff hurriedly offered
Sandy lots of new research equipment when he heard the rumors.
But by now,Sandy was deciding how she was going to set
up things at the corporate laboratory.
