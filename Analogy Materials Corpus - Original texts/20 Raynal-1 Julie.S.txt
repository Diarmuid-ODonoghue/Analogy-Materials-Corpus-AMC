
Julie is in love with Victor, her classmate, and she is getting closer to him in order to seduce him. But Diane joins the class in the middle of the year and also has a crush on Victor. Julie notices that Diane is not very aware of her style and gives her some makeover advice, showing her fashion photos and taking her out for shopping. Diane now looks very cute and chic. Diane is so grateful that she tells Julie that she will stop flirting with Victor.
