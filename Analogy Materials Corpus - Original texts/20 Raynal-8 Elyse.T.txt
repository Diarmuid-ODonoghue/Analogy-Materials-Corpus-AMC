Elyse and Charles are very happy since they got married. They have many projects and, since the birth of their first son Antoine, are considering selling their apartment to buy a house with more space. However, while using Charles' phone, Elyse found out that he has been meeting with another woman. Shocked, Elyse rushes and fills a suitcase with her clothes and leaves the house. After reflecting, she finally realizes that she has no other choice than to stay and take care of her little Antoine.
