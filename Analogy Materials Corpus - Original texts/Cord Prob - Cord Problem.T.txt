Cord Problem. 
Suppose you are in a room, where two cords are hung from the ceiling. The two cords are of such a length that when you hold one cord in either hand, you cannot reach the other. Your task is to  tie the ends of these cords together. To help you in this task, you may use any of the objects  listed below, which are also in this room: poles, clamps, pliers, extension cords, tables,  chairs.
