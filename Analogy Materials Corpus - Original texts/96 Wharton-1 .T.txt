John was very confident about himself. He did a lot of homework in order to get good marks. John had only a B+ GPA in his first year in high school. He was sure he could do better. Earlier, a counselor had arranged for him to meet with the recruiter from Yale. When he got home from class, he opened the thin rejection letter from Yale. That night he mentioned to his father how he believed that people from Ivy League schools were pretentious.
